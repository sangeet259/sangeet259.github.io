Hellooo World !

Okay So it’s already noon and I am setting up up my portfolio. While I already had a portfolio built in the summer as part of the SF Web Team Selections , but that one looked very childish and lacked professionalism. I was still sticking around with it until I saw Himanshu Mishra’s and Dibya Das’s portfolio . I was amazed that how simplistic these guys had made their portfolio. But it still needed a bit of work to be used directly. So I asked him if I can use his work , and he was kind enough :) . So now I am finishing set up with this site and leaving to study Solid Mechanics as there is a test tomorrow ! —- Tada!
